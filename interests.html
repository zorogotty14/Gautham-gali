Toastmasters, Dhwani

I participated in my local Toastmasters club, Dhwani for almost a year. I gave multiple speeches and learnt different things from a diverse community ranging from college graduates to octogenarians. I also Took up the role of Associate Vice President of Public Relations for one term where I was tasked with making posters for our weekly meeting as well as publicising our club and increasing our reach.

Link: dhwani.toastmastersclubs.org

Game Development


I was always interested in playing games and have also worked on building a few small games. I built an interactive Game of Fifteen in C during my undergraduate. I also built a basic space shooter 2D game in Stencyl. Some of my more visible work was contributing to a PyGame project as part of an initiative by IIT-Kharagpur. It gives us three attempts to find the key by telling us how close we are to the key. I worked on variable grid size, start screen and switching to SVG instead of PNG.
